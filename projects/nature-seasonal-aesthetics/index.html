10. Nature/Seasonal

Summer/Spring

Fresh, vibrant aesthetics capturing the warmth and renewal of summer and spring seasons. Features bright colors, floral patterns, and sunny motifs.

Japanese summer festival aesthetic with traditional elements like fireworks, yukatas, paper lanterns, and festival foods. Vibrant and celebratory.

Tropical/Hawaiian

Paradise-inspired aesthetic featuring palm trees, tropical flowers, ocean waves, and island vibes. Bold, colorful, and relaxing.

Early 2000s glossy aesthetic combining nature elements with technology. Features water droplets, grass, bubbles, and glass effects with vibrant blues and greens.
